## üè¢ **Property Manager Path**

*Perfect for: Property managers, leasing agents, maintenance coordinators*

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Your Dashboard">
    Log in to your Travtus account and explore the [main dashboard](/user-guide/features/dashboard-analytics)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set Up Properties">
    Add your properties and units using our [property management](/user-guide/features/property-management) tools
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start Communicating">
    Learn the [resident communication](/user-guide/features/resident-communication) features for emails, SMS, and chat
  </Step>
  <Step title="Manage Tasks">
    Create and track [maintenance tasks](/user-guide/features/maintenance-tasks) and work orders
  </Step>
</Steps>

### **Essential Features for Property Managers**
- **Dashboard Overview**: See all your properties, occupancy rates, and key metrics at a glance
- **Resident Communication**: Send targeted messages to residents via email, SMS, or in-app chat
- **Maintenance Management**: Track work orders, assign tasks, and monitor completion status
- **Reporting**: Generate reports on occupancy, financials, and operational metrics

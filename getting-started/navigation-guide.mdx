## üîç **Search & Discovery**

### **Using Search Effectively**

<Steps>
  <Step title="Use the Search Bar">
    Click the search icon or press `Cmd+K` (Mac) or `Ctrl+K` (Windows) to open search
  </Step>
  <Step title="Search Tips">
    - Use specific terms: "API authentication" vs "auth"
    - Try different keywords: "webhook" and "events" and "notifications"
    - Search for error codes: "404" or "authentication failed"
  </Step>
  <Step title="Browse Results">
    Results show page titles, descriptions, and relevant content snippets
  </Step>
</Steps>

### **Search Examples**

<Accordion title="Common Search Queries">
- **"how to add users"** ‚Üí User Management guides

## Gmail

Integrate your Gmail inbox with the Travtus platform for seamless communication and enhanced workflow visibility.

You can now integrate and manage your Gmail connection through the Travtus Studio. To begin set up:

1. Click **Connect**
2. Select **Gmail** from the systems list

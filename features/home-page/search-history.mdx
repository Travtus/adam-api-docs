**Article Overview:**

Search History allows users to **easily find and revisit your past searches**, regenerate a search and bookmark them to be easily rediscovered.

**Use Case:**

Users may recall conducting a useful search in the past but have now forgotten what the search query was.

**Step-by-Step Instructions:**

1. Select the menu bar (top right)
   - Click on **Search History** from the options
2. Analyze search history results
   - View past searches and when they were conducted
3. Select to view summary of results and initiate other actions
   - Click into results to view summary from the last time the search was conducted
   - **Regenerate** an updated search to receive up to date results
   - **Bookmark**search to easily rediscover

**Pro Tip:**

- Selecting the date range button will allow you to populate past searches within the select date range


- Using the date range selector (top of page) will allow you to regenerate search within your selected timeframe

### **SMS Compliance & Regulations**

<Warning>
**Legal Requirements**: SMS communications must comply with TCPA (Telephone Consumer Protection Act) and other regulations. Always obtain proper consent before sending text messages.
</Warning>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Obtain Consent">
    Get explicit written consent before sending SMS messages to residents
  </Step>
  <Step title="Provide Opt-Out">
    Include clear opt-out instructions in every message (e.g., "Reply STOP to unsubscribe")
  </Step>
  <Step title="Respect Preferences">
    Honor opt-out requests immediately and maintain preference records
  </Step>
  <Step title="Document Consent">
    Keep records of when and how consent was obtained for each resident
  </Step>
</Steps>

## ðŸŽ¯ **Communication Best Practices**

### **Channel Selection**

<Accordion title="When to Use Email">
**Best For**:
- Detailed information and announcements
- Official notices and documentation
- Non-urgent communications
- Marketing and community building

**Avoid For**:
- Emergency situations requiring immediate attention
- Simple yes/no questions
- Time-sensitive maintenance issues
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="When to Use SMS">
**Best For**:
- Urgent notifications and alerts
- Quick reminders and confirmations
- Time-sensitive updates
- Brief status notifications

**Avoid For**:
- Long, detailed explanations
- Official legal notices
- Complex information requiring attachments
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="When to Use Chat">
**Best For**:
- Real-time problem solving
- Back-and-forth conversations
- Maintenance coordination
- Quick questions and clarifications

**Avoid For**:
- Official record-keeping communications
- Complex policy explanations
- Formal notices and announcements
</Accordion>

## ðŸ†˜ **Troubleshooting Communication Issues**

### **Common Problems**

<Accordion title="Emails Not Being Delivered">
**Possible Causes**:
- Email addresses are incorrect or outdated
- Messages are being marked as spam
- Email server configuration issues
- Resident email filters blocking messages

**Solutions**:
- Verify email addresses in resident profiles
- Check spam folder and whitelist instructions
- Review email content for spam triggers
- Test with different email providers
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="SMS Messages Not Sending">
**Possible Causes**:
- Phone numbers are incorrect or inactive
- SMS provider account issues
- Carrier blocking or filtering
- Resident has opted out of SMS

**Solutions**:
- Verify phone numbers in resident profiles
- Check SMS provider account status
- Review opt-out lists and preferences
- Test with different phone numbers
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Low Response Rates">
**Possible Causes**:
- Messages are not reaching residents
- Content is not engaging or relevant
- Poor timing of message delivery
- Residents prefer different communication channels

**Solutions**:
- Improve subject lines and message content
- Test different sending times
- Survey residents about communication preferences
- A/B test different message formats
</Accordion>

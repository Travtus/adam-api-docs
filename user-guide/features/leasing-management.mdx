- Inadequate follow-up processes

- Implement systematic follow-up procedures

- Enhance tour training and preparation

- Inadequate marketing exposure
- Seasonal market conditions

**Solutions**:
- Streamline application and approval processes
- Implement automated decision workflows
- Expand marketing reach and channels
- Adjust pricing and concession strategies
</Accordion>
